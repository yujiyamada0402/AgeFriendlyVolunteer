Staff guide

For the Administrative Coordinator, Orientation Trainers, and MD / Volunteer Lead: the onboarding workflow at a glance, and what this site automates at each step.

Step Responsible What to do Site support
1. Initial screening Administrative Coordinator Brief interview: 100-hour commitment, availability for 3+ hour shifts, fit for the AFHS volunteer role. —
2. MD / Lead review MD / Volunteer Lead Meet the candidate, review with the coordinator, flag concerns, approve to move forward. —
3. Pre-orientation education Administrative Coordinator Set up a Sinai email if needed, then send the volunteer the link to the Pre-Orientation page. (MSQ volunteers identified by Dr. Kamal skip steps 1–2 and start here.) The Pre-Orientation page IS the packet — surveys, videos, CAPC modules, and reading in one link.
4. Verify completion Administrative Coordinator Check the completion-form spreadsheet, review, and connect the volunteer with an Orientation Trainer. Confirmation form responses collect automatically in a spreadsheet. [link to response sheet — placeholder]
5. Orientation training Orientation Trainers One Zoom session with role-playing + about three in-person sessions. Assess communication, professionalism, compassionate presence, role-specific skills, and readiness to volunteer independently. The Orientation page pre-briefs volunteers on the format and assessment criteria.
6. Orientation completion Orientation Trainers Confirm the volunteer is ready and notify the MD / Volunteer Lead that they can be placed. —
7. Volunteer assignment MD / Volunteer Lead Email the volunteer their assignment, contacts, shifts, and any additional instructions. Email template: [link or text — placeholder]
8. Program completion Administrative Coordinator After the volunteer's 100 hours, confirm they take the program post-survey and close out their participation. Post-survey is linked on the Volunteering page. [response sheet — placeholder]
Add links for staff: completion-form response spreadsheet, survey response sheets, assignment email template, and internal contact list.
Note: this page contains no confidential information — it describes the same workflow volunteers see, from the staff side. Do not add candidate names or evaluations here; keep those in internal systems.
