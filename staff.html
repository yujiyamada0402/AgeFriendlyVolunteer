For program staff

Staff guide

For the Administrative Coordinator, Orientation Trainers, and MD / Volunteer Lead: the onboarding workflow at a glance, and what this site automates at each step.

Step Responsible What to do Site support
1. Initial screening Administrative Coordinator Brief interview: 100-hour commitment, availability for 3+ hour shifts, fit for the AFHS volunteer role. —
2. MD / Lead review MD / Volunteer Lead Meet the candidate, review with the coordinator, flag concerns, approve to move forward. —
3. Pre-orientation education Administrative Coordinator Set up a Sinai email if needed, then send the volunteer the link to the Pre-Orientation page. (MSQ volunteers identified by Dr. Kamal skip steps 1–2 and start here.) The Pre-Orientation page IS the packet — surveys, videos, CAPC modules, and reading in one link.
4. Verify completion Administrative Coordinator Check the completion-form spreadsheet, review, and connect the volunteer with an Orientation Trainer. Completion-form responses appear in the form's Responses tab (Microsoft Forms) for the form owner.
5. Orientation training Orientation Trainers One Zoom session with role-playing + about three in-person sessions. Assess communication, professionalism, compassionate presence, role-specific skills, and readiness to volunteer independently. The Orientation page pre-briefs volunteers on the format and assessment criteria.
6. Orientation completion Orientation Trainers Have the volunteer complete the AFHS Volunteer Confidence Survey as the last step, then notify the MD / Volunteer Lead that they can be placed. Survey is linked on the Orientation page. Anonymous; used to correlate volunteer self-confidence with trainer evaluations. Temporary — revisit after summer 2026 once we know which parts of orientation are most helpful.
7. Sign up & start volunteering Volunteer (self-serve) After orientation, point the volunteer to the Volunteering page. They sign up for a weekday and contact their attending directly — no assignment email needed. Volunteers sign up and find contacts via the Volunteering page. Staff can see who has signed up on the sign-up list (Mount Sinai login). Optional welcome/nudge: email template below.
8. Program completion Administrative Coordinator After the volunteer's 100 hours, confirm they take the program post-survey and close out their participation. Post-survey is linked on the Volunteering page; responses appear in the form's Responses tab (Google Forms).
