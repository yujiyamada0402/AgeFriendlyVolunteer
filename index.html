Your path to volunteering

Steps marked On this site are completed right here, at your own pace. The others happen in person or by email.

  1. Initial screening

    Administrative Coordinator

    A brief interview about the 100-hour commitment, your availability for shifts of at least three hours, and fit for the role.

  2. MD / Volunteer Lead review

    MD / Volunteer Lead

    A short meeting with the program lead, who approves you to move forward.

  3. Pre-orientation education

    On this site

    Complete the pre-orientation survey, educational videos, CAPC modules, and required reading. Start here.

  4. Completion confirmation

    On this site

    Submit the completion form at the end of the Pre-Orientation page. The coordinator then connects you with an Orientation Trainer.

  5. Orientation training

    Orientation Trainers

    One Zoom session with role-playing, then about three in-person sessions. See what to expect.

  6. Confidence survey

    On this site

    Right after your last orientation session, take the short anonymous confidence survey on the Orientation page.

  7. Sign up & start volunteering

    On this site

    Sign up for your weekday and contact your attending — no waiting for an assignment email. See how to get started.

  8. Complete the program & post-survey

    On this site

    After your 100 hours, take the program post-survey on the Volunteering page to close out your participation.

MSQ volunteers identified by Dr. Kamal: you may skip steps 1–2 and begin directly with Pre-Orientation education.
