Pre-orientation education

This page is your complete pre-orientation packet. Work through the items below in order, checking each one off as you go. When everything is done, submit the completion form at the bottom — that is how the program team knows you are ready for orientation.

Need a Mount Sinai email? If you do not already have one, contact the Administrative Coordinator, Andrea Benya (andrea.benya@mssm.edu), who will set it up for you.

CAPC learning modules

The Center to Advance Palliative Care (CAPC) offers the online courses and articles below. Complete all nine items.

First, create your CAPC account (free): go to CAPC.org, click "Create Account" in the upper-right corner, and register with your Mount Sinai email address. Then click the link in the verification email from noreply@capc.org (check spam if it doesn't arrive). For account help, contact membership@capc.org.
CAPC · 9 items

Required CAPC modules

  • Empathic Response (handout)
  • Supporting the Caregivers of People with Serious Illness (course)
  • Mitigating Distress in People Living with Dementia and Their Caregivers (course)
  • Screening for Delirium: What Clinicians Should Know (article)
  • Managing Delirium: What Clinicians Should Know (article)
  • How to Prevent Delirium by Improving Sleep (article — focus on the non-pharmaceutical aspects)
  • Safe and Age-Friendly Care for Older Adults (course)
  • How to Reduce Risk for Older Adults with Age-Friendly Care (article)
  • Preparing Patients and Caregivers for Dementia Progression (course)
